What We Will Provide You

In the documents PDF, you will receive copies of all documents submitted by your agent:

  • Passport copy submitted to embassy
  • Visa rejection or acceptance letter
  • Copy of all financial documents
  • Proof of occupation documents
  • Proof of invitation (if applicable)
  • All other primary documents submitted by your agent
